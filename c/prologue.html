You're on the set of the TV show The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air. Will Smith is supposed to film the theme song today, but he has a bad cold and can't talk. You're the only one who can take his place, but you don't know the lyrics.

Will's so sick he doesn't remember the lyrics either. He knows his secretary has them written down on a piece of paper in her filing cabinet, but he can't remember her phone number.

You call 4-1-1 and ask, Could I have Will Smith's phone number? The people at 4-1-1 look up Will's number in the phonebook and connect you to 867-5309.

His secretary picks up. You say, Read me the lyrics for Fresh Prince. She goes to her filing cabinet, gets out a folder labled Songs, finds a document labled Fresh Prince, and reads it to you.

You memorize the lyrics and perform the song:
